# Norwegian Pavilion — Workflow Process Guide
## Barcelona Seafood Expo 2026

---

## Purpose

This guide defines exactly what to do at each stage of recruiting a company to the pavilion. Follow the cadence below to reduce email volume, avoid companies slipping through, and close participants faster.

---

## The 7 Status Stages

Use these exact values in the **Status** column of the tracker. Colour coding applies automatically.

| Status | Meaning | Your next action |
|---|---|---|
| **To contact** | Not yet reached out | Send Template 1 today |
| **Contacted — no reply** | Email sent, no response after 5 days | Send Template 2 |
| **In dialogue** | Two-way conversation started | Follow up within 48h of every exchange |
| **Meeting booked** | Call or meeting scheduled | Send Template 4 confirmation |
| **Proposal sent** | Pricing sheet sent | Follow up after 3 business days |
| **Contract sent** | Contract / signing link sent | Follow up after 2 business days |
| **SIGNED & PAID** | Contract signed and deposit received | Send Template 8 welcome email |

## Follow-Up Cadence

### Cold outreach
```
Day 0   → Send Template 1 (Initial outreach)
Day 5   → No reply? Send Template 2 (Follow-up 1)
Day 10  → Still no reply? Send Template 3 + attempt a phone call (last attempt)
Day 11+ → Mark as "No response — archive" in Notes. Move on.
```

### Once in dialogue
- Follow up within **48 hours** of every email or call exchange.
- If they go quiet after replying, treat as a new Day 0 and follow the cold cadence again (max 2 more follow-ups).

### Once proposal sent
- Follow up after **3 business days** if no reply.
- If still no reply after another 3 days: call them directly.
- Remind of the early bird deadline if it applies.

### Once contract sent
- Follow up after **2 business days** if not signed.
- Use the signing link (DocuSign / Docuseal) — you can see if they have opened it.
- After 5 days unsigned: call directly and offer to walk them through it live.

### Once signed — payment
- Invoice goes out **same day** as signed contract.
- Payment reminder at **3 days before due date** (Template 7a).
- If overdue: send Template 7b and call immediately.

## Hard Deadline Rule

Communicate a firm spot deadline in every proposal and contract email:

> *"We have [X] spots remaining. To secure your place, we need a signed contract by **[DATE]**. After this date we cannot guarantee availability."*

Set this deadline in the tracker's **Notes** column for every company and put it in your calendar.

## Weekly Review Routine (30 minutes, every Monday)

1. Open the **Company Pipeline** sheet.
2. Sort by **Next Follow-Up Date** (column J) — oldest first.
3. Action every company due for follow-up today.
4. Update **Last Contact Date** and **Next Follow-Up Date** for every company you touch.
5. Log every action in the **Follow-Up Log** sheet (date, what you did, next step).
6. Check the **Budget Dashboard** — are you on track to fill spots and cover costs?

## E-Signing Setup (Recommended)

Move away from PDF → email → scan → return. This alone can cut contract turnaround from days to hours.

### Option A: Docuseal (Free, self-hosted or cloud)
- Website: https://www.docuseal.com
- Free tier available (cloud). No per-envelope fees.
- Upload your contract PDF, define signature fields, send a link.
- Signer clicks link in email, signs with mouse or touch, done.
- You get a notification + signed PDF automatically.

### Option B: DocuSign (Paid, professional)
- Industry standard. Most companies recognise and trust it immediately.
- Cost: approx. USD 10–25/month for basic plan.
- Best if you send a high volume of contracts or need audit trail for large clients.

### Option C: Adobe Acrobat Sign
- Included if you already have an Adobe CC subscription.
- Similar to DocuSign in terms of flow.

**Recommendation:** Start with Docuseal (free). If companies push back or you need more features, upgrade to DocuSign.

## Key Rules (Summary)

1. **Every company has a Next Follow-Up Date.** If a cell in column J is blank or past, you are behind.
2. **Update the tracker same day** — never rely on memory.
3. **Every status change gets a log entry** in the Follow-Up Log sheet.
4. **Communicate deadlines early and repeat them** — companies move faster when the deadline is real.
5. **Phone beats email** at the proposal and contract stages. If they haven't signed in 5 days, call.
6. **Never send an unsigned contract reminder without checking the e-sign tool first** — they may have already opened it.
