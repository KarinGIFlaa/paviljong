The 8 Status Stages

Follow-Up Cadence

Cold outreach — no response yet:

  • Day 0
    Send Template 1 — initial outreach. Personalise line 2 with something specific to their company.
  • Day 5
    No reply? Send Template 2 — short follow-up. Keep it light.
  • Day 10
    Still no reply? Send Template 3 + attempt a phone call. This is your last attempt.
  • Day 11+
    No response after 3 attempts → mark as Declined / No and note "No response" in the Notes column. Move on.

Once in dialogue:

  • 48h rule
    Follow up within 48 hours of every email or call exchange. Never leave a conversation hanging.
  • Gone quiet
    If they stop replying after engaging, treat as a new Day 0 — max 2 more follow-ups before marking Declined.

Once proposal sent:

  • Day 3
    Follow up if no reply. Ask if they have questions. Mention early bird deadline if it applies.
  • Day 6
    Still no reply → call them directly. Email alone is not enough at this stage.

Once contract sent:

  • Day 2
    Follow up if not signed. Check your e-sign tool first — you can see if they opened the link.
  • Day 5
    Still unsigned → call and offer to walk through the signing live on a quick call. Takes 2 minutes.

Once signed — payment:

  • Same day
    Invoice goes out the same day as the signed contract.
  • 3 days before due
    Send Template 7a — friendly reminder.
  • Overdue
    Send Template 7b + call immediately. Do not wait.
⚠ Hard deadline rule: Communicate a firm spot deadline in every proposal and contract email — "Spots are limited. To secure your place, we need a signed contract by [DATE]." Companies move faster when the deadline is real.

Weekly Review Routine — 30 Minutes Every Monday

  • 1
    Open Company Pipeline sheet. Sort by Next Follow-Up Date — oldest first.
  • 2
    Action every company due for follow-up today. Don't skip anyone.
  • 3
    Update Last Contact Date and Next Follow-Up Date for every company you touch.
  • 4
    Log every action in the Follow-Up Log sheet — date, what you did, next step.
  • 5
    Check the Budget Dashboard — are you on track to fill spots and cover costs?
  • 6
    Any company where Next Follow-Up Date is blank or past = you are behind. Fix it now.
💡 The tracker only works if you update it the same day as every contact. Never rely on memory.

E-Signing — Move Away from PDF Email

Sending PDF → email → sign → scan → send back can take 3–5 days. A signing link takes under 2 minutes for the company. This one change alone will speed up your close rate significantly.

⭐ Recommended to start

Docuseal

Free cloud version available. Upload your contract PDF, define where to sign, send a link. Signer clicks, signs with mouse or touch, done. You get the signed PDF automatically.

Paid

DocuSign

Industry standard — most companies recognise and trust it immediately. ~USD 10–25/month. Best if you send high volumes or need a full audit trail for large clients.

Paid / Bundled

Adobe Acrobat Sign

Included in Adobe CC subscriptions. Similar flow to DocuSign. Good option if your organisation already pays for Adobe.

💡 Start with Docuseal (free). If companies push back or you need more features, upgrade to DocuSign.

6 Key Rules

  • 1
    Every company always has a Next Follow-Up Date. If column K is blank or past, you are behind.
  • 2
    Update the tracker the same day as every contact. Never rely on memory.
  • 3
    Every status change gets a log entry in the Follow-Up Log sheet — date, action, next step.
  • 4
    Communicate deadlines early and repeat them. Companies move faster when the deadline is real and visible.
  • 5
    Phone beats email at the proposal and contract stages. If they haven't replied in 5 days, call.
  • 6
    Never send an unsigned contract reminder without checking your e-sign tool first — they may have already opened it.
